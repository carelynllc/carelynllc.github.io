Care coordination
that actually works

No more group texts, sticky notes, or unanswered questions. Carelyn keeps your entire care team on the same page.

Medication Tracking

Track daily medications with RxNorm-powered search and FDA side-effect information. Log when meds are taken — everyone sees it instantly.

Care Scheduling

Coordinate who's covering care each day. Assign shifts, spot gaps before they become problems, and leave handoff notes for the next caregiver.

Health Vitals

Log blood pressure, heart rate, blood glucose, and sleep. View 7-day trends that help you spot patterns worth discussing with a doctor.

Task Coordination

Create, assign, and track care tasks across your entire team. Set priorities, due dates, and see real-time progress so nothing falls through the cracks.

Care Journal

Log daily observations, mood, and notes. Build a chronological record that becomes invaluable at doctor appointments and hospital admissions.

Family Care Circle

Invite family, friends, and professional caregivers. Everyone gets real-time updates. No more "did you hear?" conversations.
